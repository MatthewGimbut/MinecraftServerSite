Rules of the server

There are very few server-wide rules in MattCraft.

  • No intentional griefing.
  • Don't exploit glitches/bugs.
  • Autoclickers or scripts to prevent AFK kicking are prohibited.
  • Don't be an asshole.

There are no rules in place beyond these few. Build permissions and land protection are handled through the FTB Chunks mod.

See a house in an unclaimed and unprotected area? Go raid it. See someone wandering alone at night in the wilderness? Go after them. It's the wild west out there.
