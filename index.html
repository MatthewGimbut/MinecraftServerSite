Welcome to MattCraft

A modded Forge 1.15.2 server

MattCraft is a modded Minecraft Forge server focused on increasing the difficulty and complexity of the game. Building off of the default Minecraft hard difficulty, MattCraft adds a new layer of challenges through smarter and varied enemies. Now, you have to survive an onslaught of dragon attacks and mutant monsters that target your base.

The server can be found at:

  • 40.112.128.105
  • matt-server.westus.cloudapp.azure.com
MattCraft will be online 24/7 with the exception of downtime for maintenance or server backups. The server will be unavailable for about 30 minutes at approximately 05:00 UTC on all odd numbered days for routine backup.
